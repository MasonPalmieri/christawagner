About

Hi, I'm Christa

I'm a home cook, renovation enthusiast, and golden retriever mom based in Massachusetts. My fiancé and I are in the middle of restoring our early 1800s New England home — think original hardwood floors, 200-year-old character, and a whole lot of demo days.

When I'm not swinging a hammer or in the kitchen, you'll find me exploring local coffee shops, thrifting for vintage finds, or at the beach with our dog Leo. I started sharing our journey on TikTok, and this site is the next chapter — a place for recipes, renovation updates, and all the things that make this life feel good.
